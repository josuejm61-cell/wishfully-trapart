Top 5 Favorite Movies

By Josue Mondragon

The List

  1. Transformers

    IThe fate of humanity is at stake when two races of robots, the good Autobots and the villainous Decepticons, bring their war to Earth. The robots have the ability to change into different mechanical objects as they seek the key to ultimate power, but only human Sam Witwicky can save the world from total destruction.

  2. Avengers

    The Avengers are a team of superheroes and the protagonists of the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU) media franchise, based on the eponymous team from Marvel

  3. Spiderman

    Spider-Man is a superhero in American comic books published by Marvel Comics

  4. Captain Marvel

    Captain Marvel is an extraterrestrial Kree warrior who finds herself caught in the middle of an intergalactic battle between her people and the Skrulls

  5. Superman(2025)

    When Superman gets drawn into conflicts at home and abroad, his actions are questioned, giving tech billionaire Lex Luthor the opportunity to get the Man of Steel out of the way for good.
