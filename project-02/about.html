About Me

Josue J. Mondragon is a bilingual banking professional with nearly a decade of experience in customer service, financial operations, and client support. As an Associate Banker at JPMorgan Chase, he is dedicated to helping individuals and families achieve their financial goals through clear communication, personalized guidance, and dependable service. With a strong background in leadership and team management, Josue previously oversaw daily operations and staff development as a Certified Swing Manager with McDonald’s. His experience managing people, improving service processes, and maintaining operational accuracy continues to shape his customer-focused approach in the financial industry. Josue is currently pursuing his bachelor’s degree at Texas Tech University and has also volunteered in educational roles, supporting classrooms, teaching financial literacy, and engaging young students in learning. Fluent in English and Spanish, he values connection, community, and making a positive impact through his work.
